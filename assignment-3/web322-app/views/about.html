Our story

We started our store in Summer 2024 as a Tennis store for WEB222 - Assignment 04, offering a wide selection of high-quality tennis gear and accessories. Now, we have expanded our operations to serve a much broader range of needs. Our store has grown to include categories like Home & Garden, Electronics, Clothing, Sports & Outdoors, and Pet Supplies, catering to customers from all walks of life.

While our roots are in tennis, our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction remains the same as we grow. Today, we are proud to offer an extensive collection of products that fit into your everyday life—whether you’re upgrading your gadgets, shopping for new clothing, improving your home and garden, or simply finding the best products for your pets.

We thank our customers for being part of this journey and look forward to continuing to serve you with a wide range of products and unmatched customer service.
